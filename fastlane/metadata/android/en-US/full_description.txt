Dosh is a polished mobile word puzzle game for learning the Chechen language.

Drag letters from the spinning wheel to build words, fill the crossword grid, and unlock hidden bonus words. Every solved word reveals its Turkish meaning, turning each round into a tiny language lesson.

Features:
- 51 handcrafted levels with smooth difficulty progression
- Themed packs: nature, animals, food, colors, objects, concepts
- Daily challenge with coin rewards
- Bonus words hidden in the letter wheel
- 3 scenic themes: Caucasus, night, forest
- Lifetime statistics
- No ads, no in-app purchases, works offline
