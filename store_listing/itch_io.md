## Long Description
**Дош** is a polished mobile word puzzle game for learning the **Chechen language (Noxçiyn mott)**.

Swipe letters from the spinning wheel to build words, fill the crossword grid, and unlock hidden bonus words. Every solved word reveals its Turkish meaning, turning each round into a tiny language lesson.

### Features
- **51 handcrafted levels** with smooth difficulty progression
- **Themed packs**: nature, animals, food, colors, objects, concepts
- **Daily challenge**: a new level every day with a coin reward
- **Bonus words**: extra words hidden in the letter wheel
- **3 scenic themes**: Caucasus mountains, night sky, forest
- **Lifetime stats**: words solved, coins earned, hints used, best streak
- **No ads, no in-app purchases, completely free**
- **Works offline**

### How to Play
1. Look at the crossword grid and the letter wheel.
2. Drag letters from the wheel to form valid Chechen words.
3. Fill every slot in the grid to complete the level.
4. Find bonus words for extra coins.

Great for anyone curious about Chechen language and culture.
